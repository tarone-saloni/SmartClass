# SmartClass - E-Learning Platform (LMS)

## Problem Statement
Students need a centralized platform for accessing study materials and learning resources efficiently and effectively.

## Project Description
SmartClass is a comprehensive Learning Management System (LMS) designed to enhance digital learning and provide flexibility in education. The platform serves as a bridge between educators and learners, enabling seamless content delivery and progress tracking.

## Key Features

### For Teachers
- **Upload Study Materials**: Share video lectures, notes, and documents with students
- **Create Assignments**: Design and distribute assignments to track student progress
- **Manage Content**: Organize and update learning resources easily
- **Monitor Progress**: Track student engagement and performance metrics

### For Students
- **Access Learning Materials**: Download or stream lectures, notes, and resources anytime, anywhere
- **Track Progress**: Monitor personal learning journey with progress indicators
- **Submit Assignments**: Complete and submit assignments directly on the platform
- **Participate in Quizzes**: Take assessments and receive instant feedback
- **Flexible Learning**: Learn at your own pace with 24/7 access to materials

## Benefits
- **Centralized Learning Hub**: All resources in one accessible location
- **Digital Transformation**: Modernizes traditional education delivery
- **Enhanced Engagement**: Interactive content and assessments boost learning outcomes
- **Flexibility**: Access materials anytime, anywhere
- **Progress Tracking**: Real-time insights into student performance
